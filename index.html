About Me

With 13 years of experience in the IT industry, I’ve journeyed through various technical roles, starting as an apprentice and advancing through help desk and on-site support. Over time, I moved into infrastructure and systems management, eventually transitioning into project delivery and management. Today, I serve as a Security Manager, leading our 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) team, which I had the privilege of building from the ground up. I’ve designed and implemented all policies, workflows, and standards for the team, ensuring smooth operations and consistent performance.

Throughout my career, I’ve developed and integrated numerous monitoring solutions across customer environments, linking them seamlessly with tools like Microsoft 365. In my current role, I continue to focus on automation, leveraging PowerShell to streamline and optimize tasks for my team, enhancing efficiency and reducing manual effort.

I’ve successfully managed and delivered large-scale projects for notable clients, including London Array, North Air, SGN, Prada, and FM Conway, ensuring each project was completed to the highest standard.

Outside of work, I am an avid tabletop wargamer and a passionate painter and modeller. I enjoy creating 3D models using Blender and frequently handle print requests from my local game store community.
